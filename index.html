BC Mission Trip

An outreach initiative by the MFSDAC to assist the children and youth of the Gitxsan nation in Kitwanga, British Columbia, Canada. This year, our youth department is the platform that bridges the gap between our youth and the Gitxsan youth by building a bus shelter for the children and youth of the Gitxsan nation. To learn more about the Gitxsan, visit SULADS Canada.

Gala Dinner

A fundraising event in efforts to raise funds to cover the expenses of the undergoing of our upcoming mission trip. We are striving to host this gala to raise funds to be able to provide a highly-needed youth shelter for the needs of the Gitxsan children and youth.
